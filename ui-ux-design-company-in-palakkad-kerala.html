Frequently asked question .

Here are some of our most frequently asked questions and answers about Ui Ux Designing.

The biggest difference between UI/UX design and web design is that web design focuses on what a website looks like and UI/UX design focuses on what the experience of using a site or app feels like. The ultimate goal of UI/UX design is to build something that doesn’t only look “nice,” but changes a user’s desired behavior and decision-making process.

All UI/UX engagements start with a kick-off meeting over the phone or in person. During this meeting, we’ll learn more about your organization, its stakeholders, and its goals. After we gather these requirements, we’ll create user personas, conduct competitive analysis, and move into wireframing your site or application. A full UI/UX process, inclusive of designs, usually takes about 2-3 Weeks.

Our UI/UX team designs websites, custom applications, and mobile applications.

Our team starts all UI/UX projects with thorough user research and competitive analysis. We’ll create personas of your ideal users, draft “red routes” of actions we’d like them to take while on the website, and then engage in user and A/B testing to see whether they are performing the correct behaviors.

The average cost of our UI/UX engagements vary depending on the complexity and requirements of your engagement. Contact us to known about the cost of UI/UX design.
